Pillows and cushions are long-view items.
They sit in shared spaces and are seen repeatedly, which makes visual fatigue a real buying concern.

Minimal symbolic art works better here because it supports calm, coordination, and longevity without dominating the room.

## Why soft furnishings punish noise

Busy or novelty designs feel tiring on items meant for rest.
On pillows and cushions, visual noise becomes more noticeable over time.

Restrained symbolic designs:
- stay comfortable to live with
- remain readable at close range
- avoid clashing with surrounding decor

## Structure over pattern on fabric

Fabric softens edges and spreads contrast.
Patterns that rely on precision often lose clarity.

Symbolic forms with clear structure:
- hold shape when softened
- remain legible under folds
- feel intentional rather than decorative
